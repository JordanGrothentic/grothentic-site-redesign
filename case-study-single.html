The Challenge

Great care, invisible online.

Victory Performance PT provided exceptional physical therapy services in their local market, but their digital presence was holding them back. Despite having stellar in-person reviews, their website was severely outdated, slow to load, and invisible on Google for "Physical Therapist near me" or specific injury treatments.

They were relying entirely on word-of-mouth referrals, leaving massive local search volume to their competitors.

Before working with us, Victory Performance PT's site was converting less than 1% of visitors.
Our Approach

A localized, revenue-first strategy

1
Technical Rebuild

We migrated them from a bloated WordPress theme to a custom, lightning-fast setup. Core Web Vitals scores went from failing to 98/100 across mobile and desktop, immediately reducing bounce rates.

2
Service Area SEO

Instead of just targeting "Physical Therapy", we built dedicated, optimized landing pages for specific treatments (Sciatica, ACL rehab) localized to their exact surrounding zip codes.

3
GBP Optimization

We completely overhauled their Google Business Profile. We implemented a consistent review-generation strategy and localized Q&A schemas that helped them dominate the Local Map Pack.

The Results

Dominating the local map pack.

Within 90 days, the technical improvements and localized content strategy triggered a massive surge in visibility. They moved from page 4 to the top 3 Map Pack results for their most profitable keywords.

More importantly, this traffic converted. By implementing clear CTAs and a frictionless booking system on the new site, monthly qualified leads jumped from nearly zero to 47.
